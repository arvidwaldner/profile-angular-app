Experience

With over 9 years of experience in .NET software development, Arvid brings knowledge and expertise to every project. His hands on experience comes from both .NET Framework, .NET Core and newer versions of .NET.

Rest API Passion

Quality Assurance Mindset

Arvid has solid experience of automated unit testing and integration testing through different frameworks such as MSTest, Xunit and Moq. Even experience of automated UI tests with Playwright has been gained.


Humble Attitude

Arvid has been appreciated for his humble attitude and growth mindset. He's open to feedback and always eager to learn and improve.

Code Reviews

Cloud

AI Prompting Skills

Continous Learning

Diverse Business Industry Experience

CI/CD
